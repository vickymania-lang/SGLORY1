Brief History

Welcome to our school

SUCCESS GLORY ACADEMY SCHOOL was established in September 2012 And it was established base on situation on ground in the community at that time because many children are not going to school as they cannot afford some of the school fees at that time so we started as a mission school with a fee as low as 3,500 and with the help of our committed teachers a lot of pupil have graduated from our school into higher school
